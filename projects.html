Foresight

Consulting Project Manager & TC Developer

Sep 2025-Dec 2025

Foresight is an AI-driven hotel revenue intelligence startup that leverages event data and market analytics to help hotels optimize pricing and occupancy in real time. As the Consulting PM, I led a five-member team in researching market dynamics, competitive positioning, and channel partnerships to inform the startup’s go-to-market strategy. As a TC Developer, I collaborated with the development team to prototype a data pipeline for event-based demand forecasting, integrating APIs from hospitality and event data sources. My work focused on translating business goals into data-backed insights, strengthening the product’s strategic positioning within the hospitality technology landscape.

Key Contributions

  • Designed and deployed a client-facing visualization dashboard using D3.js.
  • Managed a multi-disciplinary consulting team and timeline under a six-week sprint model.
  • Presented findings to the client’s executive board with actionable insights supported by visual analytics.
